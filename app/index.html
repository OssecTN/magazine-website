Conventus is released

It is with great pleasure that we release the 3rd Number of the OSSEC magazine entitled "Conventus". As always , the magazine tries to bring you a variety of information and news concerning open source , brought to you by various article writers coming together from different places and engineering schools .. That's the spirit : open source unites us all

The OSSEC team would like to take this opportunity to thank all the contributers for their great work and we hope we're getting closer to our readers's expectations ..

We were pleased to receive your feedback on the last issue and we would like to hear from you this time as well , so remember that your opinion matters to us and send us an e-mail !

Have a pleasant reading !
